# Voice Commands Quick Reference

## How to Activate
1. Click the **🎤 Voice Navigation** button in sidebar
2. Button turns red → Ready to listen
3. Speak a command
4. System navigates automatically

---

## Tips & Tricks

✨ **Speak naturally** - Clear pronunciation works best
⏱️ **Wait for confirmation** - System speaks back when ready
🔊 **Check volume** - Make sure speaker volume is on
🎙️ **Good microphone** - Built-in mics work fine
🤫 **Quiet environment** - Works better in quiet areas

---

## What to Say

| ✅ Good | ❌ Avoid |
|--------|---------|
| "resources" | "go to resources" |
| "mentors" | "show me mentors" |
| "dashboard" | "take me home" |
| "ai assistant" | "open the ai thing" |
